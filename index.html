Russell Westbrook

Biography

Russell Westbrook is an American basketball player born on November 12, 1988 in Long Beach, California. He attended the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) before being drafted into the NBA by the Seattle SuperSonics in 2008.

Westbrook is known for his explosive play and versatility, able to score, pass and grab rebounds. He won the 2017 NBA MVP award and is considered one of the best players of his generation.
